FAHLUZ

jewelry with a cause

We're partners,and we hand-craft all of our sparkling jewelry just for you. The name we chose for our joint effort is FAHLUZ, which is the sound of the first syllables of each of our middle names strung together like beads on a string: Kathie Faye + Christy Louise.

So why do we say we make jewelry with a cause? After my brain surgery in 2001 I had to learn everything over, and Christy thought that working with my brain and hands together for coordination would help tremendously with my rehab.

Today we continue making our jewelry for the joy of it, and we wish you joy in wearing it. -- Kathie

We can work with you to create a personalized, custom, one-of-a-kind design.
